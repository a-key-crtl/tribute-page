Alan Turing

The father of modern computer science

Here's a time line of Alan Turing's life:


  • 1912 - Born in Maida Vale, London.

  • 1926 - At age of 13, Turing first showed signs of intelligence and potential noticed at Sherborne School.

  • 1931 - Studies from 1931-1934 at King's College, in Cambridge. First in his class and an honor student in field of mathematics.

  • 1936 - Studies mathematics and cryptology at Princeton University

  • 1940 - Creates Bombe which was a cryptanalytic machine used to decrypt the Enigma-enciphered messages being sent from enemies.
